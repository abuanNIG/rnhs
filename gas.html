GAS

This strand is purposively design for those student who are still indecisive of what course or degree they want to pursue in college. A student who finish on this strand can proceed to any college program. However he/she has to taken some subjects not taken in Senior High but required by a particular college as a birdging program to asses his/her preparedness in the field of study chosen.
